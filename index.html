Circadian Rhythm

Your body operates on a 24-hour internal clock governed by light exposure. Disrupting it — through late nights, artificial light, or irregular schedules — impairs hormone regulation, immunity, metabolism, and cognitive function. Aligning your life to this rhythm is the single highest-leverage health intervention available to you.

Head & Neurological
Headache
Types & Causes

Tension headache (muscle contraction), Cluster headache (vascular), Sinus headache (inflammation), Hormonal headache (hormonal fluctuation), Dehydration headache, Hypertension headache.

Symptoms

Dull or throbbing pain, pressure around forehead or temples, sensitivity to light or sound, nausea (in severe cases), neck stiffness.

Global Natural Remedies

🇮🇳 Ayurvedic: Brahmi oil scalp massage, ginger-tulsi tea. 🇨🇳 TCM: Peppermint oil at LI4 acupoint, chrysanthemum tea. 🇯🇵 Japanese: Warm towel compress, mugwort steam. 🌿 Western herbal: Feverfew, magnesium glycinate. General: Hydration, cold compress, dark quiet room.

Diet — Include
Magnesium-rich foods Ginger tea Water (2.5–3L) Leafy greens Almonds
Diet — Avoid
Processed foods Alcohol Excess caffeine Aged cheese MSG
⚕️ When to See a Doctor

If headache is sudden and severe ("thunderclap"), accompanied by fever and stiff neck, causes vision changes, follows head injury, or does not respond to any treatment after 72 hours.
